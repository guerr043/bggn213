How would I plot this with ‘ggplot2’?

ggplot(cars)

Every ggplot needs at least 3 layers:

-Data (i.e. the data.frame we have), -Aes (the asethetic mapping of our data to what we want to plot) -Geoms (How we want to plot this stuff!)
